What is the true indication of a great physique? Is it large, peaked biceps? Is it razor-sharp abs? Is it bowling-ball shoulders or a wide thick back?


None of those are correct. The real answer is, symmetry.

A "Symmetrical Physique" is one in which all body parts flow into each other. No one body part or side of the body is bigger than the other.
